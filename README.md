# 👨‍💼 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze employee data and understand **attrition patterns, workforce distribution, and key HR metrics**.

The goal is to help organizations:
- Reduce employee attrition  
- Understand workforce trends  
- Improve employee retention strategies  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### 🔹 Key KPIs

- 👥 **Total Employees:** 1470  
- 📉 **Total Attrition:** 237  
- 📊 **Attrition Rate:** 16.12%  
- 🎂 **Average Age:** 36.9 years  
- 💰 **Average Salary:** 6.5K  
- 🏢 **Average Years at Company:** 7.0  

---

## 📈 Key Visualizations

- 📊 **Attrition by Education Field**  
- 👶 **Attrition by Age Group**  
- 💰 **Attrition by Salary Slab**  
- 👔 **Attrition by Job Role**  
- 🚻 **Attrition by Gender**  
- 🏢 **Department-wise Attrition (HR, Sales, R&D)**  

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- Highest attrition occurs in the **26–35 age group**  
- Employees with **lower salary slabs** show higher attrition  
- Certain roles like **Sales Executive and Laboratory Technician** have higher attrition  
- Male employees show higher attrition compared to female employees  
- Education field influences employee retention patterns  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve retention strategies for **mid-age workforce (26–35)**  
- Review compensation for **lower salary brackets**  
- Focus on high-attrition roles with targeted HR policies  
- Enhance employee engagement programs  
- Optimize hiring and retention strategies based on department trends
